About Us

Our Medical Team.

We take care of families as a whole, not just our patients, with loving and personalized care. Our dedicated staff including our Registered Nurses, Medical Assistants, and Office Managers, are eager to help you with questions relating to your children’s health and immunizations.

Dr. Rajkumar

He received his undergraduate degree in Biochemistry with honors (summa cum laude) from Columbia University in New York, NY.  He received his medical degree from Columbia University College of Physicians and Surgeons in New York, NY in 1986. ​ Dr. Rajkumar completed his residency in Internal Medicine at St. Luke's Hospital (New York, NY) and proceeded to obtain a fellowship in Gastroenterology from SUNY- Downstate (Brooklyn, NY). ​ After finishing fellowship, he started his practice in Internal Medicine and Gastroenterology in South Plainfield and Perth Amboy. He became the Chief of Gastroenterology at Muhlenberg Hospital.
